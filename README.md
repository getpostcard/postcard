# Postcard

Open-source firmware and tooling for hackable e-paper devices, starting with the Seeed
reTerminal E1003. It runs a device as a picture frame and a static information panel: a
rotating set of your photos, plus pages of reference information you author yourself,
such as contacts, Wi-Fi credentials, and procedures. No cloud, no accounts, no telemetry.

## Status

Very early. There are no releases, and nothing here is stable yet. The structure,
interfaces, and design decisions will change as the project develops.

## AI assistance

Postcard is written with AI assistance. AI is used as a tool under human direction. Every
change is reviewed and understood by a human before it is committed, and unreviewed AI
output is not accepted. AI-assisted commits are attributed in the commit history. See
AGENTS.md.
